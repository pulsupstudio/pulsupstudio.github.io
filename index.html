Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23.12.2024

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect and process data to ensure the functionality of the app, display personalized ads, and analyze user behavior to improve the application.

1.1 Personal Data

The app does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, or phone number.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

During the use of the app, we may automatically collect the following data:

  • Device type (model, OS version).
  • Region, language, and device time zone.
  • Frequency of using app features.

This data is used exclusively for analytics and to improve the app.

1.3 Analytics

We use third-party analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to understand how users interact with the app. These services may collect data on:

  • Number of app launches.
  • Usage of specific features.
  • Time spent in the app.

Data is collected anonymously and does not include any information that identifies you.

1.4 Advertising

The app uses third-party advertising networks (e.g., Google AdMob) to display ads. Advertising platforms may collect and use information about your device, including:

  • Unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID).
  • Preferences based on your behavior within the app.

Personalized ads are delivered based on this data.

1.5 In-App Purchases

In the future, the app may include paid features or products. Payments will be processed through the Google Play platform. We do not store or process your bank card or other payment information.

2. Usage of Collected Data

The collected data is used to:

  • Ensure the functionality of the app.
  • Display ads and analyze their performance.
  • Improve functionality and user experience.
  • Analyze data to identify issues and opportunities.

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share data with the following third parties:

  • Advertising platforms (e.g., Google AdMob) – to deliver personalized ads.
  • Analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics) – to analyze app usage.
  • Payment systems (e.g., Google Play) – to process in-app purchases.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We take measures to protect the collected data from unauthorized access, alteration, and deletion. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Decline to use the app if you disagree with the data collection terms.
  • Contact us for additional information or to request the deletion of collected data (where possible).

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. In the event of significant changes, we will notify users by updating the text within the app or on our website.
